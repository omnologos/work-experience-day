How Alexa works

  1. Wake word: A small model listens locally for the wake word.
  2. Record & send: The device records your request and sends it securely to the cloud.
  3. Cloud processing: Speech-to-text and NLU map the request to an intent.
  4. Action: Alexa replies, plays media, or commands smart devices.

Common uses

  • Get quick answers, timers, and reminders
  • Control smart home devices with voice
  • Play music, podcasts, or radio
  • Use Skills for games, shopping, and utilities
View products Alexa Developer
  • Alexa Skills are voice apps that extend capabilities.
  • Wake-word detection runs locally; full processing happens in the cloud.
  • Routines let you chain commands to a single phrase.
  • Alexa integrates with many smart-home brands.
  • Use the Alexa app to review and delete voice history.

Privacy & controls

You can mute the mic, review and delete recordings, and control Skill permissions in your Alexa account.
